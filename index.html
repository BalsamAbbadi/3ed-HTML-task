Segafredo restaurant

Segafredo Café in Tulkarm is a unique destination for coffee lovers and a quiet atmosphere.
Located in the Shuweika suburb, it offers a wide variety of hot and cold drinks with snacks
that suit everyone's tastes. The café has a modern atmosphere and comfortable service that
suits individuals and families alike. With its central location and multiple payment options,
Segafredo is an ideal place to relax or meet friends. Flexible daily working hours make it
a unique haven at any time of the day.
